About AI Dialogue Aggregation

AI Dialogue Aggregation is a free online multi-window dashboard and navigation tool that helps you open multiple frequently-used websites in a single browser tab. Whether it's AI chat, search, social media, video, shopping, or daily tools, you can freely mix and match to build your own efficient workspace.

Use Cases

  • AI Comparison: Open Doubao, Kimi, Baidu, and other AI platforms side-by-side to compare responses.
  • Info Aggregation: Search, browse news, and check maps simultaneously in one page.
  • Quick Navigation: Pin 4 or more frequently-used sites into one panel and stop switching tabs back and forth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?

Yes, AI Dialogue Aggregation is completely free to use. No registration or login is required.

Will my conversation content be recorded?

No. This site is a purely static website and all pages run locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or analyze any of your conversation content. Please read our Privacy Policy for details.

Why do some AI sites show "refused to connect"?

Some AI platforms prohibit third-party websites from embedding them via iframes for security reasons. You can click the "Open in new tab" button in the top-right corner of any card to use them in a standalone tab.

How do I add a custom AI website?

Click the "+" button at the end of the grid, or click the gear icon in the top-right corner of any card to select a preset platform or enter a custom URL.

How to Use

🖥️ Add a window

Click the "+" button at the end of the grid, then pick a preset site from the categorized list (AI Chat / Search / Social & Video / Shopping / Tools) or enter a custom URL.

⚙️ Change site

Click the gear icon in the top-right corner of any card to switch that window to another preset or custom URL. Other windows stay untouched.

↔️ Drag to reorder

Drag a card by its blank area (avoid buttons) and drop it onto another card to swap positions. Loaded content in other cards will not refresh.

◢ Resize

Drag the diagonal handle at the bottom-right corner of a card to resize it freely. The layout adapts automatically without overlapping.

↗️ Open in new tab

Some websites block iframe embedding for security and may show "refused to connect". Click the "↗" icon to open them in a standalone tab instead.
