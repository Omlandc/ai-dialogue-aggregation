Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

AI Dialogue Aggregation ("the Site") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We follow the principle of minimal data collection. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or other personal identity information
  • Any conversation content you enter on third-party AI platforms (such as DeepSeek, Kimi, Doubao, etc.)
  • Your login credentials or cookies for third-party AI platforms

We only use localStorage in your browser to store your custom window configurations (e.g., the list of AI sites you have added). This data is stored entirely on your local device and is never uploaded to our servers.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Site itself does not set any cookies. If you encounter cookies related to advertising (such as Google AdSense) in the future, these are set by third-party advertising service providers based on your browser settings. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

3. Third-Party Services and Links

The Site embeds multiple third-party AI chat platforms via iframes or external links. These platforms have their own privacy policies and terms of service, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We recommend that you review the respective platforms' privacy policies before use.

4. Data Security

Since the Site is a purely static website, all pages are transmitted over HTTPS. We do not operate a backend server, so there is no risk of server-side data breaches.

5. Your Rights

You can delete all locally stored configuration data for this Site at any time by using your browser's "Clear site data" feature.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page. We recommend that you review it periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the links in the site footer or the channel through which you accessed the Site.
